Mobile Phones


Iphone

The iPhone, launched in2007 by Apple, revolutionized smartphones with its touchscreen and iOS ecosystem. Designed in California and manufactured by Foxconn and Pegatron, it offers premium build, powerful performance, and strong security. Each new model brings innovations in cameras, AI, and user experience, making it one of the most popular smartphones globally.


Samsung

Samsung phones are known for innovation, premium design, and powerful cameras. The Galaxy series, launched in 2010, introduced AMOLED displays and foldable technology. Manufactured in South Korea, Vietnam, and India, they feature in-house Exynos chips and One UI, keeping Samsung a global leader.


Realme

Realme, founded in 2018, is known for budget-friendly smartphones with powerful features. It quickly gained popularity with high-performance processors, fast charging, and stylish designs. Manufactured in China, India, and other key markets, Realme focuses on affordability and innovation.


Motorola

Motorola, founded in 1928, pioneered mobile communication and launched the first mobile phone in 1983. Known for durable and innovative designs, it gained fame with the Razr series. Now owned by Lenovo, Motorola manufactures smartphones in China, India, and Brazil, focusing on reliability and performance.


Google Pixel

Google Pixel, launched in 2016, is Google's flagship smartphone series known for its AI-powered camera, clean Android experience, and timely updates. Initially produced by HTC, later models were manufactured by Foxconn and other partners. With the introduction of its in-house Tensor chip, Google optimized Pixel devices for AI, security, and performance. The Pixel lineup focuses on premium software integration, advanced photography, and a smooth user experience.
